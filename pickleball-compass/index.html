Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Designed for real pickleball players — fast to log, smart to analyze.

🏓

Fast Score Logging

Record a game in seconds. Enter four players, scores, and you're done. Log from your iPhone, iPad, or Apple Watch.

📊

Player Stats & Streaks

See win rates, records, and trends for yourself and every player you've faced. Track win streaks, days played streaks, and milestone wins.

⌚

Apple Watch App

Log games right from your wrist without pulling out your phone. Stats sync instantly using your iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch.

❤️

Health Integration

Optionally pull in heart rate and workout data from Apple Health to see how your body performs on big days.

📅

Schedule Calendar

See your pickleball calendar events alongside your recorded games — color-coded so you always know what's coming and what's been played.

📖

Rules Reference

Full pickleball rules reference built right in — serving, kitchen, faults, scoring, and more. Includes a link to the official USA Pickleball rulebook.

🎓

Training Videos

Curated beginner videos covering rules, serving, kitchen play, and common mistakes — all watchable without leaving the app.

☁️

iCloud Sync

Settings stay in sync across all your devices automatically. No account required.

🏸

Paddles & Balls Guide

Browse top paddle brands and models with type and level tags, plus the leading ball brands — with direct links to each company's site.

💾

Backup & Restore

Export your game history to JSON or CSV and import backups — perfect for switching devices or keeping an archive.

🏆

Leagues

Create and manage leagues with full standings, weekly results, rosters, and win/loss records for every player.

Pro Subscription

Level up with Pro.

Everything in the Basic plan, plus powerful tools for serious players.

🎬

Strategy & Drill Videos

Curated strategy and drill video libraries — third shot drops, stacking, dinking, footwork, and more — all in-app.

📝

Training Plan Notes

Keep dated notes on what you want to work on. Build your own training journal over time.
