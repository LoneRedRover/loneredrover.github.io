Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Pickleball Compass ("the App") is developed and operated by Lone Red Rover. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Data We Collect

Game Data

The App stores the game scores, player names, dates, and locations you enter. This data is saved locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and may sync to your personal iCloud account if you have iCloud enabled.

Health Data

With your explicit permission, the App reads workout, heart rate, HRV, and sleep data from Apple HealthKit to display alongside your game history. This data is read-only and is never transmitted to Lone Red Rover or any third party. It stays on your device and in your personal Health app.

Location

The App may use your approximate location to suggest a venue name when logging a game. Location is used only in the moment you log a game and is never stored beyond the location label you choose to save.

Settings & Preferences

App settings (such as your name, preferred win score, and display preferences) are stored locally and may sync via iCloud to your other devices. These are personal preferences and are not shared with Lone Red Rover.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect any analytics or usage data.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or tracking frameworks.
  • We do not have servers that receive your personal data.
  • We do not sell or share any information with third parties.

iCloud

If you use iCloud, your game data and settings may be stored in your personal iCloud account, governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. This storage is private to your Apple Account and encrypted by Apple — Lone Red Rover has no access to it and could not read your data if we wanted to. Syncing is optional: you can turn it off for this app at any time in iOS Settings › your name › iCloud › Saved to iCloud, and the App will keep all of your data on-device only.

Apple HealthKit

Health data accessed through HealthKit is used solely to display information within the App. It is never used for advertising, shared with third parties, or uploaded to any server.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
