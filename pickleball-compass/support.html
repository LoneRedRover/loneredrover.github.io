Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a game?

Tap the + button on the main screen. Enter the four player names, the final scores, and tap Save. The game appears instantly in your history.

Can I log games from my Apple Watch?

Yes. Open the Pickleball Compass app on your Watch, tap New Game, and follow the prompts. The game syncs to your iPhone automatically.

How do I edit or delete a game?

In your game history, swipe left on any game to reveal the Delete option, or tap the game to open it and use the Edit button.

Why isn't heart rate or workout data showing up?

The App reads from Apple Health with your permission. Go to Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Pickleball Compass and make sure the relevant categories are enabled. You'll also need to have recorded a workout in Apple Health around the time of the game.

How does player stats work?

The App tallies wins, losses, and ties for every player name that appears in your game history. Make sure you spell names consistently across games for accurate stats. You can toggle the stats section on and off from Settings, or shake your phone to quickly toggle it.

Will my data sync across my devices?

App settings sync via iCloud automatically. Game data is stored locally on the device where it was recorded. If you want your history on a new device, use the export feature to back it up first.

Is there a subscription or in-app purchase?

No. Pickleball Compass is a one-time purchase with no subscriptions, no ads, and no in-app purchases.

How do I change the win score (11, 15, or 21)?

Tap the gear icon to open Settings and change the Default Win Score. You can also override it per game when logging.
