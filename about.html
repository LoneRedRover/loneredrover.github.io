Lone Red Rover

Lone Red Rover is a one-person indie studio run by Ron McCamish — a recently retired Software Architect who spent his career building mobile and cloud software. When you've spent decades shipping apps for other people, eventually you build one for yourself.

Two years ago, Ron discovered pickleball, got hooked fast, and built an app to track it. What started as a way to log scores became something bigger: a tool to track your history, understand your game, and see how you're improving over time.

Designed in RTL and Dublin, Ohio.
